What is it?

A smart academic
companion app

UniTrack – Smart Academic Dashboard brings together three essential tools into one clean, dark-themed web app designed for students who want to stay on top of their academic life without juggling multiple apps or spreadsheets.

Everything runs in your browser using localStorage, so your tasks, lectures, and GPA data are always there when you come back — no account, no server, no fuss.

How to use

Simple steps to get started

Every module is self-explanatory — here's a quick overview.

01
Task Manager

Keep track of every assignment, project, and deadline in one place. Never miss a submission again.

  • 1 Go to the Dashboard and scroll to the Task section.
  • 2 Click "+ Add Task", enter the task name, pick a deadline date and choose a priority level (High / Medium / Low).
  • 3 Hit Add. Your task appears instantly in the list — colour-coded by priority.
  • 4 Tick the checkbox to mark it done, or click the trash icon to delete it.
  • 5 Use the filter tabs (All / Pending / Done) to quickly switch views.
Open Tasks
02
Lecture Timetable

Build your full weekly schedule in minutes. The app highlights today's column automatically every time you visit.

  • 1 Scroll to the Timetable section on the Dashboard.
  • 2 Click "+ Add Lecture" to open the form.
  • 3 Fill in the subject name, select the day (Mon – Fri), enter the start & end time, and optionally add a room/venue.
  • 4 Click Save — the lecture slots in to the correct day column, sorted by time.
  • 5 Delete any lecture with the × button on that card.
Open Timetable
03
GPA Calculator

Instantly see your weighted GPA as you add subjects. A colour-coded result card tells you exactly where you stand.

  • 1 Go to the GPA section on the Dashboard.
  • 2 Click "+ Add Subject".
  • 3 Enter the subject name, the credit hours, and pick the grade from the dropdown (A, B+, B, C+, C, D, F).
  • 4 Hit Add — your GPA recalculates instantly using a weighted average.
  • 5 The result card turns green (≥ 3.5), orange (≥ 2.0), or red (< 2.0) based on your score.
